Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 April 2026

Overview

KRVR is a PC VR streaming application. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the data we handle.

Data We Collect

KRVR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No accounts — KRVR does not require sign-up or login.
  • No analytics — We do not track usage, sessions, or behaviour.
  • No advertising — There are no ads or ad-related tracking.
  • No cloud storage — All settings are stored locally on your devices.

Network Communication

KRVR streams video, audio, and controller data between your Windows PC and Apple Vision Pro over your local network. This data:

  • Never leaves your local network
  • Is not sent to any external server
  • Is not logged or recorded by KRVR

Third-Party Components

KRVR uses NVIDIA CloudXR for streaming. CloudXR may communicate with NVIDIA servers for licensing and telemetry as part of its standard operation. Please refer to NVIDIA's Privacy Policy for details.

KRVR uses Apple's ARKit and FoveatedStreaming frameworks. Eye tracking data used for foveated encoding is processed locally on the Apple Vision Pro and is not transmitted to any server.

Bonjour / mDNS

KRVR uses Bonjour (mDNS) for local network device discovery. This broadcasts a service name on your local network so the Vision Pro can find your PC. No data is sent outside your network.

Children's Privacy

KRVR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
