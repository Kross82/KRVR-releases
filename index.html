Features

⚡

CloudXR Streaming Live

Powered by NVIDIA CloudXR with Apple FoveatedStreaming. Eye-tracked foveated streaming renders full resolution where you look, reduced detail in the periphery. Works with any OpenXR game.

👁

Foveated Streaming

Eye-tracked foveated rendering powered by Apple FoveatedStreaming and CloudXR. Full detail where you look, reduced periphery. Same total bandwidth, dramatically better perceived quality.

🎯

Game Profiles

Per-game resolution, quality, and streaming settings. Auto-applied when you launch a game from the built-in library. Dial in the perfect balance for each title.

📷

Cockpit Passthrough

Draw a passthrough window around your real steering wheel, HOTAS, or cockpit hardware. Trace the shape with your finger, and that area becomes a real-world cutout in VR. Saves automatically between sessions.

🕹

PSVR2 Sense Controllers

Full 6DOF tracking and input with PlayStation VR2 Sense controllers on visionOS 26. Trigger, grip, joystick, buttons, and haptic feedback.

Requirements

Windows PC

  • NVIDIA GeForce RTX 40 series or newer
  • Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • Latest NVIDIA drivers
  • 5GHz WiFi, 50Mbps+ sustained

Apple Vision Pro

  • visionOS 26.4 or later
  • TestFlight app installed
  • Same local network as PC
  • WiFi 6 recommended

Optional

  • PSVR2 Sense controllers
  • Dedicated WiFi 6 access point
  • PC wired to router via Ethernet
  • OpenComposite for OpenVR games

Troubleshooting

  • Sunshine / Moonlight conflict — Close Sunshine before running KRVR. Both use overlapping UDP ports (47998-48012). Sunshine must be fully stopped or KRVR won't connect.
  • First connection fails — Run KRVR as administrator once, then restart normally. The installer sets permissions but some antivirus software can block it.
  • Black screen after connecting — Make sure you launch the game after the session is connected (green status). Some games need OpenComposite to work with CloudXR.
