Features

⚡

CloudXR Streaming Live

Powered by NVIDIA CloudXR with Apple FoveatedStreaming. Eye-tracked foveated encoding sends full resolution where you look, compressed periphery. Works with any OpenXR game.

👁

Foveated Encoding

NVENC QP Delta Maps concentrate bitrate at the centre of your vision. Same total bandwidth, dramatically better perceived quality. Eye tracking driven in CloudXR mode.

🎯

Game Profiles

Per-game resolution, quality, and streaming settings. Auto-applied when you launch a game from the built-in library. Dial in the perfect balance for each title.

📷

Passthrough & Mixed Reality

Alpha blending support for cockpit sims. See your real desk and peripherals while racing or flying. Auto-switches between passthrough and full immersion.

🕹

PSVR2 Sense Controllers

Full 6DOF tracking and input with PlayStation VR2 Sense controllers on visionOS 26. Trigger, grip, joystick, buttons, and haptic feedback.

Requirements

Windows PC

  • NVIDIA GeForce RTX 40 series or newer
  • Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • Latest NVIDIA drivers
  • 5GHz WiFi, 50Mbps+ sustained

Apple Vision Pro

  • visionOS 26.4 or later
  • TestFlight app installed
  • Same local network as PC
  • WiFi 6 recommended

Optional

  • PSVR2 Sense controllers
  • Dedicated WiFi 6 access point
  • PC wired to router via Ethernet
  • OpenComposite for OpenVR games
